
# User Guides for Over The Top (OTT)

I'll create comprehensive text-based guidelines that you can share with your host and players. These cover the complete flow from setup to scoring.

---

## HOST GUIDE

### Getting Started as a Host

**Step 1: Request Access**
1. Go to the OTT homepage
2. Click "Request Access"
3. Fill out the form with your name, email, and select "Group" as your play style
4. Choose your expected group size (2-5 or 6-10 players)
5. Optionally tell us where you heard about OTT
6. Wait for approval from the Commissioner (you'll receive an email)

**Step 2: Claim Your Party**
1. Once approved, you'll receive a unique **Party Code** (4-6 characters like "RUMBLE" or "ABC123")
2. Click the link in your approval email or go to the Join page
3. Create an account or sign in with your email and password
4. You'll automatically be assigned as the host of your party

**Step 3: Access Your Party Dashboard**
1. After signing in, go to **My Dashboard** (therumbleapp.com/my-parties)
2. You'll see your party listed under "My Hosted Parties" with a crown icon
3. Click on your party code to access **Host Setup**

---

### Setting Up Your Party (Pre-Event)

**Host Setup Dashboard Overview:**
- **Event Status Card** - Shows "Pre-Event" status, number of guests joined, and how many have completed their picks
- **My Picks Card** - Track your own picks (yes, hosts can play too!)
- **Guests List** - See all players who have joined and their pick completion status

**Sharing with Guests:**
- Share your **Party Code** with friends (displayed in the header)
- Guests can join at: therumbleapp.com/join
- They enter the code and create an account to join

**Making Your Own Picks:**
1. Click "Edit" on the My Picks card
2. You'll enter the pick-making flow just like any other player
3. Complete all 36 picks across matches, Rumble props, and chaos props
4. Click "Save" at any time to return to the Host Setup

**TV Mode (Optional):**
- Click "TV Mode" to open a display optimized for big screens
- Cast this to a TV during your watch party
- Shows live leaderboard, entry grids, and scoring updates

---

### Starting the Event

**When to Start:**
- Wait until all guests have joined and completed their picks
- You need at least 2 players to start
- The "Start Event" button shows guest count requirements

**What Happens When You Start:**
1. Click "Start Event" in the footer
2. Rumble numbers (1-30) are randomly distributed among all players for both Men's and Women's matches
3. Players see a reveal animation showing their assigned numbers
4. The party status changes from "Pre-Event" to "Live"
5. All picks become locked - no more editing allowed

---

### Live Event Scoring (Host Control)

**Navigating Host Control:**
After starting, you're redirected to the Host Control panel with four tabs:
- **Matches** - Score undercard matches
- **Props** - Score chaos prop bets
- **Men's** - Manage Men's Rumble entries and eliminations
- **Women's** - Manage Women's Rumble entries and eliminations

Use the arrow buttons at the bottom to navigate between tabs.

---

**Scoring Undercard Matches:**
1. Go to the "Matches" tab
2. For each match, tap the winner's name
3. Points are automatically awarded to players who predicted correctly (25 points per correct pick)
4. A green checkmark shows the match is scored

**Scoring Chaos Props:**
1. Go to the "Props" tab
2. Each prop is a Yes/No question (e.g., "Did a tag team betray each other?")
3. Tap "Yes" or "No" for each prop as events happen during the Rumble
4. 10 points awarded per correct prediction

---

**Managing the Rumble (Men's / Women's tabs):**

**Entering Wrestlers:**
1. When a new entrant appears, find their number in the grid
2. Tap "Set Wrestler" and select who entered
3. If it's a surprise entrant not in the list, use "Add Surprise Entrant"
4. The timer starts tracking their time in the ring

**Recording Eliminations:**
1. When someone gets thrown out, tap "Eliminate" on their card
2. Select who eliminated them (or "Self" for accidents)
3. Players earn 5 points for each elimination by wrestlers they own

**Final Four Bonus:**
- When only 4 wrestlers remain, a confirmation dialog appears
- Review the Final Four and confirm
- Points awarded to players who correctly predicted any of the four finalists

**Declaring the Winner:**
- When only 1 wrestler remains, tap "Declare Winner"
- This triggers:
  - 50 points to players who picked this wrestler as winner
  - 50 points to the player who owns this number
  - Iron Man/Woman bonus (20 points) for the player owning the wrestler with longest time
  - Celebration animations for players involved

---

### After the Event

- The leaderboard shows final standings
- Players can view their complete results on their dashboards
- TV Mode displays the final leaderboard
- You can access the event history anytime from My Dashboard

## PLAYER (GUEST) GUIDE

### Joining a Party

**Step 1: Get the Code**
- Your host will share a 4-6 character Party Code with you

**Step 2: Join the Party**
1. Go to therumbleapp.com/join
2. Enter the Party Code your host gave you
3. Tap "Join Party"

**Step 3: Create Your Account**
1. Enter your display name (this shows on the leaderboard)
2. Enter your email address
3. Create a password (6+ characters)
4. Tap "Create Account & Join"

**Already have an account?**
- Switch to the "Log In" tab
- Enter your email and password
- You can use the same account across multiple parties

---

### Making Your Picks

**The Pick Flow:**
After joining, you'll go through a card-based pick flow. Complete all 36 picks:

**Undercard Matches (2 picks):**
- Drew McIntyre vs Sami Zayn
- AJ Styles vs Gunther
- Tap the wrestler you think will win

**Rumble Winner (2 picks):**
- Men's Royal Rumble Winner
- Women's Royal Rumble Winner
- Scroll through the list and tap your prediction

**Rumble Props (18 picks total, 9 per gender):**
For both Men's and Women's Rumble:
- **#1 Entrant** - Who enters first?
- **#30 Entrant** - Who enters last?
- **First Eliminated** - Who gets thrown out first?
- **Most Eliminations** - Who eliminates the most wrestlers?
- **Longest Time** - Who lasts longest in the match?
- **Final Four** (4 picks) - Pick 4 wrestlers you think make the final four

**Chaos Props (14 picks, 7 per gender):**
Yes/No predictions on wild moments:
- Floor is Lava - Someone uses a stunt to avoid elimination
- Revolving Door - Someone eliminated in under 10 seconds
- Betrayal - Tag partners or allies eliminate each other
- Over/Under Surprises - More than 2.5 surprise entrants
- Giant Slayer - Takes 3+ wrestlers to eliminate someone
- Brought a Toy - Someone brings a weapon into the ring
- Left on Read - Music plays but no one appears

**Navigation:**
- Use "Back" and "Next" buttons to move through cards
- Tap the "Save" icon anytime to save progress and exit
- A progress bar shows how many picks you've completed

---

### Your Dashboard

**Accessing Your Dashboard:**
1. Sign in at therumbleapp.com/sign-in
2. Go to My Dashboard
3. Click on your party to view your picks and stats

**Dashboard Sections:**
- **Header** - Shows your total points and rank
- **Numbers Tab** - Your assigned Rumble entry numbers (visible after event starts)
- **Matches Tab** - Your undercard and winner picks
- **Men's Tab** - Your Men's Rumble prop picks
- **Women's Tab** - Your Women's Rumble prop picks
- **Chaos Tab** - Your chaos prop picks

**Editing Picks (Pre-Event Only):**
- Before the event starts, tap the pencil icon on any pick to change it
- After the host clicks "Start Event", all picks are locked

---

### During the Event

**Number Reveal:**
- When the event starts, you'll see an animation revealing your assigned numbers
- You get numbers for both Men's and Women's Rumble matches
- You earn points when wrestlers with your numbers eliminate others

**Live Updates:**
- Your dashboard updates in real-time as the host scores
- Watch your points grow as you get predictions right
- Green checkmarks show correct picks

**Celebrations:**
- If your number makes the Final Four, you'll see a celebration overlay
- If your number wins the Rumble, an even bigger celebration appears
- If you predicted the winner correctly, you earn 50 bonus points

### Troubleshooting

**"I forgot my password"**
- On the sign-in page, tap "Forgot password?"
- Enter your email to receive a reset link

**"I can't find my party"**
- Go to therumbleapp.com/my-parties after signing in
- Your parties appear under "Parties I've Joined"

**"My picks aren't saving"**
- Make sure you have an internet connection
- Tap the save icon before leaving the picks flow
- If the event has started, picks are locked

**"I missed the number reveal"**
- Go to your dashboard and check the "Numbers" tab
- Your assigned numbers are always visible there
